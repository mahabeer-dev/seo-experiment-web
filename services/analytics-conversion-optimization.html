Analytics & Conversion Optimization

A measurement-focused service page designed to help you practice GA4 event tracking, goal configuration, and CRO experiments.

Build a measurement plan for organic growth

Use this page as the anchor for discussions about how to measure the impact of SEO work. You can experiment with messaging that connects rankings and traffic to leads, pipeline, and revenue.

Experiment with GA4 events and conversions

The global JavaScript file pushes events such as form_submission and button_click into the dataLayer. When you swap in your own GA4 Measurement ID and GTM container, you can transform those events into conversions, audiences, and funnel steps.

For extra practice, point some events to this page as the conversion destination by using a CTA that links to the Contact page and tracking the full path from blog view to form submit.
