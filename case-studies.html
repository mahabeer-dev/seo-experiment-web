SEO Case Studies

Three fictional, but realistic, stories that demonstrate how technical SEO, content, and analytics combine to drive sustainable organic growth.

Case studies you can reference in proposals and landing pages.

SaaS Platform: From Patchy Rankings to Product-Led Organic Growth

B2B SaaS • Technical SEO + Content Strategy

A mid-market SaaS company relied heavily on paid search. By consolidating cannibalized blog posts, improving Core Web Vitals, and mapping features to problem-focused landing pages, they built a defensible organic growth engine.
+164% non-branded organic traffic +48% organic sign-ups Sitewide LCP: 4.1s → 2.3s

Local Services: Owning the Map Pack in Competitive Metro Areas

Local SEO • Service Area Business

A multi-location home services company struggled with inconsistent name, address, and phone data and weak Google Business Profiles. A structured citation clean-up, location page overhaul, and review generation program helped them dominate local packs in key cities.
+92% calls from GBP listings 3x increase in direction requests Top 3 for 75% of priority queries

Ecommerce: Turning Search Traffic into Repeat Customers

Ecommerce • CRO & Content

An ecommerce brand attracted steady organic traffic but converted poorly. By improving product detail pages, adding comparison content, and implementing GA4-powered experimentation, they strengthened both first-time and repeat purchases.
+37% organic conversion rate +22% average order value +41% returning customers
