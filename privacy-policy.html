Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

RankRocket Digital is a fictional SEO and analytics agency website created as a sandbox environment for testing on-page SEO, technical SEO, schema markup, tracking setups, and conversion experiments. This means the site is not intended for real commercial use, but it can still process both simulated and real user data when you visit and interact with it.

1. Who operates this site

For the purposes of this sandbox, “RankRocket Digital” refers to the project owner or SEO professional using this repository to experiment with search optimizations and analytics implementations.

2. What information this site collects

The site is designed to demonstrate typical data collection patterns used on real-world marketing websites. Depending on how you configure hosting and tracking, the site may collect:

  • Technical information such as IP address, browser type, device type, and approximate location derived from your connection.
  • Usage data such as pages viewed, links clicked, scroll depth, and time spent on specific content.
  • Form data that you voluntarily provide in contact or demo request forms (name, email, company, and any message you enter).

In many setups, this data is routed only to your own Google Analytics 4 property or other analytics tools that you configure rather than being stored by the project author.

3. Cookies and tracking technologies

The site is structured to support cookies and similar technologies for analytics, A/B testing, and personalization, including:

  • Analytics cookies to understand aggregate site usage and measure campaign performance.
  • Event-based tracking using the dataLayer for Google Tag Manager and GA4.
  • Optional experiment cookies if you configure A/B tests on headlines, CTAs, or layouts.

Exactly which cookies are set depends on how you configure your analytics, tag manager, and testing tools in your deployment.

4. How information is used

In a typical deployment, information collected through this sandbox may be used to:

  • Demonstrate how to measure SEO-driven traffic and conversions in GA4 or similar tools.
  • Analyze how design changes, internal linking, or content updates influence engagement.
  • Test and document example funnels, audiences, and reports for educational purposes.

The project is not intended to be used for collecting sensitive personal data such as payment details, national identifiers, or protected health information.

5. Data sharing

Any real analytics data generated on your hosted copy of this site is controlled by you through the providers you configure, such as:

  • Google Analytics 4 and Google Tag Manager
  • Other analytics or marketing tools you connect

The project author does not automatically receive or access the data from instances you host unless you explicitly grant access to your analytics accounts or logs.

6. Data retention

Data retention settings are managed in your analytics tools. For GA4, you can configure retention windows for event data and user-level information. Review those settings to ensure they align with your own compliance requirements.

7. Your choices and controls

How visitors exercise control over their data depends on how you deploy this sandbox. In a production scenario, you may want to:

  • Provide a consent banner for cookies where required by law.
  • Offer a way to opt out of analytics or marketing tracking, such as a toggle or link to a preference center.
  • Document how users can request access to, correction of, or deletion of their personal data.

8. International considerations

Because this site may be deployed globally, you are responsible for ensuring that your use of this sandbox complies with applicable privacy regulations, including but not limited to GDPR, CCPA, LGPD, and other regional frameworks.

9. Changes to this privacy policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes in the site, the tools used for analytics, or applicable regulations. The “Last updated” date at the top of this page will change when significant updates are made.

10. Contact

If you are using this project and have questions about how to adapt this privacy policy for your own deployment, you can add your preferred contact details and processes here. For example, you might include a dedicated privacy email address or contact form link.
