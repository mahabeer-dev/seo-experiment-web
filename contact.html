Conversion tracking playground

Submit a lead form and inspect the dataLayer event.

Use this form to practice building GTM triggers, GA4 conversions, and marketing-qualified lead definitions. The form never hits a backend; it simply pushes a structured event into dataLayer.

Complete the fields below, submit, and then open your browser console to inspect window.dataLayer. Look for the form_submission event with lead details.

This dropdown is captured as service_interest in dataLayer.

Use this field in GA4 to practice lead scoring and segmentation.

Ideas for GA4 and GTM experiments using this page.

  • Configure a GA4 conversion event that fires on form_submission with service_interest as a parameter.
  • Create an audience of users who clicked the contact button on the home page but did not submit this form.
  • Build a funnel that tracks organic sessions from a blog post through to this contact page and form completion.
  • Test different CTAs in the navigation and header, then compare click and completion rates.
